# I. The Research & Design Phase
## SEPTEMBER 2025 - Research & Logic

    Started deep-dive research into mechanical keyboard mounting styles (Gasket vs. Tray).

    Analyzed the RP2040 Datasheet to understand pin mapping.

    Studied the Keyboard Matrix logic to plan the 4x5 layout.

## OCTOBER 2025 - Electrical Engineering

    Began schematic design in KiCad.

    Integrated the MCU, USB-C components, and the Rotary Encoder into the circuit.

    Finalized the diode-per-switch logic to ensure NKRO (N-Key Rollover).

## NOVEMBER 2025 - PCB Layout & Routing

    Spent the month physically routing traces on the PCB.

    Designed the "ZYYY 4KPAD" aesthetic silkscreen for the back of the board.

## DECEMBER 2025 - 3D Mechanical Modeling

    Began 3D modeling the enclosure in Autodesk Fusion.

    Designed custom "scooped" ergonomic keycaps and the encoder knob.

# II. The Crisis & Pivot
## JANUARY 8, 2026 - The SSD Disaster

    22:58 PM: Catastrophic hardware failure. The laptop fell and broke; the internal SSD fried.

    Result: Loss of all original KiCad files, Fusion models, and raw video footage.

## JANUARY 9, 2026 - Emergency Mentorship

    Met with Mr. Porczek to assess the damage.

    He helped boost confidence and helped plan the "Challenging 2-Week Completion" recovery phase.

    Switched all project files to a dedicated External SSD for safety.

## JANUARY 12 - 15, 2026 - Rapid Re-Design

    Redid the entire KiCad schematic and PCB layout from memory.

    Attempted to order parts from JLCPCB; discovered RP2040 and Component Shortfalls.

    Decision: Pivoted to a full 3D-printed functional prototype to meet the school deadline.
